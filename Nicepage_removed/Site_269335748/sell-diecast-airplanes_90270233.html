Frequently Asked Questions​

​What types of items does Templar Toys buy?

​We buy all types of items from categories including Electric Model Trains, Military Model Kits, Military Diecast Toys, Vintage Toys, Toy Models and more. Please contact us at knightstemplartoys@gmail.com and let us know what you are interested in selling. We will walk you thru the steps so we can either decide it doesn't fit or move forward with getting you a quote. 

​Does Templar Toys offer cash for "any" items?

​Although we buy a large number of different items, you may be surprised to hear that there are some items and collections that just don't makes sense for us to buy. If your item is not a fit for us, we will try and be helpful to give you some information on other ways to sell your items. 

​How can I tell if Templar Toys will be interested in my collection?

​ The easiest way to contact us at knightstemplartoys@gmail.com. When you email us, we need to determine what you are selling meets our buying guidelines. 

​What is Market Value vs Wholesale Pricing?

​Market Value is what your items will typically be purchased for at an auction or online marketplace. We base our buy cost for your items that meet our buying criteria on wholesale cost.  That wholesale value takes into account selling cost "storage, local and government taxes, time, fees, etc..." and profit after the item is sold to a collector.

​Should I provide photos of my items I would like to sell?

Yes, photos of your items are very helpful in determining current market value so we can put together a cash quote quickly. Using our email address knightstemplartoys@gmail.com will be the easiest way to send photos. If you could provide images of your collection, we can work from there to get a quote. If you have an itemized list of your items that would even be better.

​How do I receive my cash offer?

After we have all the details of your item(s) that include, photos, inventory lists, condition etc... we will determine the current market value of your items. After determining the market value of your vintage toys, we will reach out to make a no obligation cash offer. If we find the details provided concerning your collection do not meet our buying guidelines, we will let you know.

Do you appraise collections?

At this time we do not do appraisals.
